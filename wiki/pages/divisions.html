Welcome to iGEM Toronto, where innovation meets collaboration in the dynamic field of synthetic biology. Our team is composed of diverse divisions, each contributing a unique set of skills and expertise to drive the success of our cutting-edge projects. Discover the essence of each division below:

{{ subheading("Wetlab Team") }}

Dive into the heart of scientific exploration with our Wetlab Team. Passionate scientists and bioengineers collaborate to conduct hands-on experiments, perfecting protocols, and bringing biological projects to life in the laboratory. If you have an interest in practical applications and precision, the Wetlab Team is the place for you.

{{ subheading("Drylab Team") }}

Unleash the power of computation in our Dry-lab Team. This division focuses on the digital frontier of synthetic biology, analyzing data, modeling complex systems, and employing bioinformatics tools. Join us if you are enthusiastic about computational biology and want to explore the limitless possibilities of the virtual world.

{{ subheading("Hardware Team") }}

Merge the realms of biology and technology with our Hardware Team. Innovative minds in electronics and hardware development collaborate to design and construct hardware solutions for synthetic biology projects. If you have a passion for building and creating tangible solutions, the Hardware Team awaits your expertise.

{{ subheading("Human Practices Team") }}

Explore the ethical, social, and cultural dimensions of synthetic biology with our Human Practices Team. Engage with diverse stakeholders, conduct research on the societal implications, and integrate responsible practices into project design. If you're interested in shaping the ethical landscape of synthetic biology, join us.

{{ subheading("Entrepreneurship Team") }}

Bridge the gap between science and business in our Entrepreneurship Team. This division identifies market opportunities, develops business plans, and explores funding strategies to transform scientific projects into impactful ventures. Join us if you are passionate about entrepreneurial success in the scientific and biotech sectors.

{{ subheading("Outreach Team") }}

Share the excitement of synthetic biology with the community through our Outreach Team. This division plans and executes educational programs, engages with schools and community organizations, and utilizes various platforms to spread awareness. If you are passionate about science communication and community engagement, the Outreach Team is your stage.

{{ subheading("Finance Team") }}

Ensure the financial success of our projects with the Finance Team. Responsible for budgeting, financial strategy, and fundraising efforts, this team plays a vital role in sustaining iGEM Toronto's initiatives. If you have a knack for numbers and strategic planning, join us in shaping the financial future of synthetic biology.

Join iGEM Toronto and be a part of a collaborative community pushing the boundaries of synthetic biology. Whether you are driven by laboratory experiments, computational exploration, hardware innovation, ethical considerations, entrepreneurial pursuits, community outreach, or financial strategy, there's a place for you to contribute to our exciting projects.
