{{ subheading("About Us")}}

iGEM Toronto is a pioneering team at the forefront of synthetic biology research and innovation. As participants in the International Genetically Engineered Machine (iGEM) competition, we aim to tackle global challenges through the application of cutting-edge biological solutions. Our team is dedicated to fostering creativity, collaboration, and scientific excellence.

Since 2007, we have been representing the University of Toronto at the undergraduate level. Our hard work has culminated in 4 gold medals, 4 silver medals, 4 bronze medals, and many special prizes. This year, our team won the award for Best Model, as well as the Gold Medal, and 5 other special nominations!
